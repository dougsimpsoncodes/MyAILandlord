Enhancement #1: Color Contrast Fix

WCAG 2.1 AA Compliance for Accessibility

WHY THIS MATTERS: The current text colors don't meet WCAG 2.1 AA standards (minimum 4.5:1 contrast ratio for normal text). This affects users with visual impairments and can be a legal liability. The fix darkens secondary/subtle text colors to ensure readability.
