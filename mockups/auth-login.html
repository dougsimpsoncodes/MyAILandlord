Auth Screens

Unified Auth

Login and Sign Up are tabs on ONE screen, not separate pages. Reduces code duplication by 50%.

Clean Branding

Logo and tagline establish identity without overwhelming. Focused on the task at hand.

Simple Form

Just the essentials: email and password. Additional fields only shown when needed (Sign Up).

Social Options

Google sign-in as alternative. One tap access for users who prefer it.

Role Selection

Sign Up includes role picker (Landlord/Tenant). Determines their experience after auth.
