Enhancement #4: Bottom Sheet for Dropdowns

Native Mobile Pattern for Better UX

WHY THIS MATTERS: Full-screen modals for simple selections feel heavy and disorienting on mobile. Bottom sheets are the iOS/Android standard for secondary selections - they keep context visible, feel lighter, and can be dismissed with a swipe. Users can see where they came from and maintain their mental model of the form.
