## Risk Mitigation

### Top Risks & Countermeasures

**Risk 1: Clinics don't convert leads**
- **Mitigation:** Pre-qualify leads heavily (insurance, location, seriousness)
- **Mitigation:** Provide lead nurturing templates to clinics
- **Mitigation:** Only work with clinics that have good reviews/track record

**Risk 2: Google algorithm change kills SEO**
- **Mitigation:** Diversify traffic (ads, social, referrals)
- **Mitigation:** Build brand recognition (not just SEO)
- **Mitigation:** Focus on helpful content, not just keywords

**Risk 3: Low traffic/high customer acquisition cost**
- **Mitigation:** Start with Google Ads (guaranteed traffic)
- **Mitigation:** Use EMD advantage for faster organic rankings
- **Mitigation:** Content marketing compounds over time

**Risk 4: Payment disputes with clinics**
- **Mitigation:** Crystal clear contract on what's a "qualified lead"
- **Mitigation:** Use third-party tracking (call recording, form submission logs)
- **Mitigation:** Offer trial period (first 5 leads free to prove value)

**Risk 5: Competitor launches same model**
- **Mitigation:** First-mover advantage (lock in best clinics)
- **Mitigation:** Build moat through content + backlinks
- **Mitigation:** Create exclusive partnerships (no competing directories)
